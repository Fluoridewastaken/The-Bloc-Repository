﻿[Automated Bulletin]
Subject(s): Cross-Floor Communication
Affected Sector(s): All Floors

Citizens, you are encouraged to participate in the new, experimental cross-floor communication system. The system can be accessed by entering the following command into any authorized communication terminal running Sys7.2.3 or higher:

ent --gg --/ Y99WJVw

Participating in the communication system allows citizens to qualify in a ration testing lottery that sends three (3) extra rations of an unreleased menu. The current experimental menu contains 0.023%* more produce from the Hydroponics Department.

*Percentage is within the statistical margin of error
